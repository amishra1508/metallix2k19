Treasure Hunt (Talaash)

Not all technical fests delve into the technicalities of science. We at Metallix 2k18, would like to introduce to you all Talaash, an event that tests your presence of mind and the thinking skills and how you deal with puzzles in the nick of a time. Experience the wildest treasure hunt you've ever experienced, one that knows no boundaries. An unending maze. So, let the game begin and may the victorious emerge.

Rules and Regulations :

1. Each team comprises of three members.
2. Students from different colleges may form a team with one member from Jadavpur University compulsory in every team. We will help in arranging for the compulsory JU student for non JU teams.
3. No help should be taken from other teams or other people.
4. Each team will have to find a certain number of clues within a stipulated amount of time.
5. First team to find all the clues will be declared the winner.
6. Use of vehicle is prohibited.
7. In case of any discrepancy, the decision of the organisers shall be final and binding.
