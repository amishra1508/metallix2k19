Poster Presentation (Scribe)

"The eye should learn to listen before it looks" - Robert Frank.
Man learnt to draw before he could write, because the better he visualizes the quicker he learns. A picture sails through a sea of texts and Metallix 2k18 gives you a platform to unveil the neoteric ideas to be put up in the wall. We gladly present to you Scribe, the Poster Presentation. Bring to light your knowledge here as we actively wait for your participation.


Rules and Regulations :

1. Participants will be required to submit an abstract of maximum 4 pages on their original scientific research or review articles based on:
i) Digitization of Metals, Oil and Power sector.
ii) Trending topics of Metallurgical and Material Science.
2. The abstract should be submitted latest by 1st March 2018.
3. The presentation should last a maximum of 7 minutes followed by 5 minutes of question-answer based on the presentation.
4. Abstracts are to be mailed at metallixju@gmail.com.
